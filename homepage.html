SAHRMS Homepage

Welcome to Shippensburg Area Hospital Record Management System!
This record management system keeps track of patients, prescriptions, and payments.
It also has a handy administrator and user manual section.
Within the patient page, you'll be able to view patients, search, add, and edit records.
In the prescription page, you will be able to order prescriptions for patients and send it to their local pharmacy.
In the payment page, you will be able to initiate payments for patients.
